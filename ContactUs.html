Doctors consultation by appointments. We have 4 types of appointments:
Routine appointments: Can be booked ahead for up to one month.
On-the-day appointments: We reserve about 20% of our appointments empty for on the day to ensure problems developed overnight will be handled on the day. These appointments are released by our computer from 8am.
Emergency appointments: Available at any time during surgery hour. If you are having an emergency or in distress then we will attend to your need on arrival without any appointments required.
After-hour appointments: (Weekdays) In case all appointments are taken for the day, our doctors will stay back after they have finished their booked appointments and attend to any extra appointments required or requested by you. Due to limited resources, your medical needs will be attended to by the first available doctor. After-hour appointments start from 5pm.
