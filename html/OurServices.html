Occupational Health

Workrecover is a branch of Lurnea Medical Centre. It is a work injury management program dedicated towards rapid treatment and safer, more productive return to work.
Workrecover provides exceptional care to injured workders through:
  •  Experienced doctor and qualified staff.
  •  Treatment priority for injured workers. We will strive for a waiting time of less than 30 minutes.
  •  Flexible appointment system allowing workers to see doctors after work to minimise work disruption.
  •  Priority referral to treating specialists, allied health practitioners, and radiology for rapid diagnosis and treatment.
  •  Improved communication for return to work with employer and
  •  Workplace rehabilitation coordinator.
Other services provided by workrecover include: Pre-employment examination, workplace vaccination, Insury Management Consultant review and report, Independent Medical Examination and report, Rehabilitation services, Railcorp medicals, Trucksafe medicals, and OHS support.

Sleep Management Australia

Sleep Management Australia is a sleep clinic with the following services:
  • Diagnostic home sleep test for Obstructive Sleep Apnoea.
  • Technical consultant services for sleep CPAP/APAP machines and all accessories.
Sleep Clinic operates at our Elizabeth Drive Medical Centre. A GP's referral is required. A Home Sleep test costs only $50.
Obstructive Sleep Apnoea is a dangerous snoring condition that can lead to heart attack, stroke, sudden death, motor vehicle accidents, hypertension, and depression.

Laverty Pathology

We have on-site pathology service provided by Laverty Pathology. Laverty Pathology is one of the most established and respected pathology company in NSW.
Pathology tests are usually bulk-billed. There are occasional cost involved in a specialised pathology test that is not covered by Medicare.
Your pathology results are downloaded directly to your doctors’ computers within 24 hours after the test. Urgent results can be obtained within the same business day.
If your abnormal result is serious and life-threatening, your doctor will receive the result after-hours to ensure that emergencies are appropriately managed.
